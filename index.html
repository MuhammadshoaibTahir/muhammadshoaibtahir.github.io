I have a background in linguistics with hands-on research experience in Natural Language Processing. My work includes the development of NLP tools and linguistic resources, as well as model analysis and evaluation, particularly for low-resource languages such as Shahmukhi Punjabi. I am interested in research that integrates linguistic theory with computational methods and values collaborative approaches to language technology.

Current Research Interests

  • NLP model development and assessment

  • Morphology and lexical semantics
  • Low-resource and multilingual NLP
  • Integration of linguistic theory and computational methods
