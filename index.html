Muhammad Shoaib Tahir

I am seeking a PhD opportunity in Computational Linguistics and Natural Language Processing, with interests in NLP model development, model evaluation and assessment, and research on low-resource languages. I am particularly motivated to integrate computational methods with linguistic insights and to collaborate on interdisciplinary research.

I have a background in linguistics with hands-on research experience in Natural Language Processing. My work involves the development of NLP tools and linguistic resources, as well as model analysis and evaluation, particularly for low-resource languages such as Shahmukhi Punjabi. I am interested in research that integrates linguistic theory with computational methods, and I value collaborative, interdisciplinary approaches to language and language technology.
