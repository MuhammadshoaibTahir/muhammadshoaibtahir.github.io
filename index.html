About

I am a computational linguist with a background in corpus linguistics and the development of linguistic resources for low-resource languages. My research focuses on linguistically informed evaluation of language models, with particular attention to morphology and lexical semantics in under-resourced languages such as Shahmukhi Punjabi.

Research Interests

  • Linguistic evaluation of language models
  • Corpus-based diagnostic methods
  • Morphology and lexical semantics
  • Low-resource and multilingual NLP
  • Language models as models of language learning

Research Projects

Punjabi WordNet
Development of a WordNet-style lexical semantic resource for Shahmukhi Punjabi by expanding synsets and semantic relations from English WordNet.

Morphological Analyzer for Punjabi
A rule-based morphological analyzer designed to capture inflectional patterns in Shahmukhi Punjabi and support corpus-based linguistic analysis.

Part-of-Speech Tagger for Punjabi
A corpus-driven, rule-based POS tagger developed for Shahmukhi Punjabi to support syntactic and morphological research.

Rule-Based Stemmer for Punjabi
A deterministic stemmer aimed at morphological normalization for corpus processing in a low-resource setting.
