About Me

I am a computational linguist with extensive expertise in Natural Language Processing, corpus linguistics, and linguistic resource development. I hold an M.Phil. in Applied Linguistics from Government College University Faisalabad, where my thesis focused on developing Punjabi Synset using expansion approaches from English WordNet under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Muhammad Asim Mahmood.

My research focuses on developing computational tools and resources for low-resource languages, particularly Punjabi, with applications in morphological analysis, POS tagging, and WordNet development. I have published over 10 research papers, authored 2 books, and delivered 13+ invited talks at prestigious institutions across Pakistan and internationally.

Currently, I serve as a Visiting Lecturer at Government College University Faisalabad and University of Education Lahore, teaching courses in Corpus Linguistics, Computational Linguistics, Morphology, and Computer Assisted Language Learning. I am also actively involved with the Linguistic Association of Pakistan and Pakistan Corpus Studio, contributing to corpus development and language resource planning at the national level.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical linguistics and computational applications, and I am eager to contribute to cutting-edge research in multilingual NLP, corpus-driven linguistics, and language technology for underrepresented languages through a PhD program.

Research Interests

Corpus Linguistics

Large-scale corpus design, annotation, and analysis for linguistic research and NLP applications. Specialized in corpus-based morphological studies and statistical analysis.

Computational Linguistics & NLP

Development of NLP tools including morphological analyzers, POS taggers, and parsing systems for low-resource languages with focus on Shahmukhi Punjabi.

Low-Resource Language Processing

Creating linguistic resources and computational tools for Punjabi and South Asian languages, addressing the digital divide for underrepresented languages.

Morphology & Syntax

Computational modeling of morphological systems, syntactic structures, and agglutinative language processing with applications in Universal Dependencies.

Lexical Semantics & WordNet Development

Specialized in WordNet development, semantic networks, lexical database construction, and cross-lingual mapping for South Asian languages.

Multilingual Sentiment Analysis

Research in cross-language sentiment detection, emotion trajectory analysis, and sentiment classification for multiple languages using NLP techniques.

Lisan City - Pakistani Languages

A futuristic 3D visualization of Pakistani languages and dialects using Three.js. Languages are represented as buildings in an interactive linguistic cityscape.

View Project

Punjabi POS Tagger

Rule-based and statistical Part-of-Speech tagger for Punjabi language supporting both Shahmukhi and Gurmukhi scripts with high accuracy across diverse text genres.

3

Punjabi WordNet

Comprehensive lexical database for Punjabi organized by semantic relations, containing synsets, hypernyms, hyponyms, meronyms, and cross-lingual links.

4

Morphological Analyzer for Punjabi

Finite-state morphological analyzer that segments words into morphemes and provides detailed morphological information including roots, affixes, and inflections.

5

Rule-Based Stemmer for Punjabi

Lightweight stemming algorithm that reduces Punjabi words to their root forms, optimized for information retrieval and text mining applications.

6

Punjabi Verbs Python Package

Python package providing comprehensive verb conjugation and analysis tools for Punjabi language processing and computational linguistics research.

7

English X-bar Tree Generator

Automated tool for generating X-bar syntactic tree structures for English sentences, useful for teaching and research in generative syntax.

8

Emotion Trajectory Analysis App

Application for analyzing emotional patterns and trajectories in text data using sentiment analysis and visualization techniques.

9

Corpus Generator System

Automated system for corpus collection, cleaning, and annotation with support for multiple languages and text sources.

10

Multidimensional Analysis Tagger

Tool for multidimensional corpus analysis based on Biber's framework, supporting register variation and text type analysis.

Secure Password Manager
